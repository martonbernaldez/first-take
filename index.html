About Tech - Talk DOIT Publication

Tech-Talk DOIT is the official school publication of DOIT College, created to give students a voice and a platform where they can express their ideas, creativity, and opinions. It serves as a space where important school events, achievements, and updates are shared with the whole community. Through this publication, students are able to stay informed and connected with what is happening inside and outside the campus.

The main purpose of Tech-Talk DOIT is to promote awareness, critical thinking, and communication among students. The club encourages learners to write about topics that matter to them—whether it’s technology, school news, student life, or social issues. By being part of the publication, students develop important skills such as writing, editing, research, teamwork, and leadership, which are useful both in school and in future careers.

More than just a publication, Tech-Talk DOIT is a community. It brings together students who are passionate about storytelling, journalism, and digital media. The club aims to inspire creativity and build confidence by giving every student a chance to share their voice. Through teamwork and dedication, Tech-Talk DOIT continues to create meaningful content that represents the spirit, talents, and values of DOIT College.
